Continuing Medical Education

CME Requirements

Virginia ophthalmologists must complete 60 hours of Category 1 CME every 2 years. NVAO events provide high-quality accredited education.

  • AMA Category 1 Credits available
  • Online tracking and certificates
  • Specialty-specific content

Annual CME Calendar

Plan your continuing education with our comprehensive annual calendar of educational events and credit opportunities.

  • Monthly clinical meetings (2 credits each)
  • Annual symposium (6 credits)
  • Specialty workshops (2-4 credits)

Online CME Portal

Access online continuing education modules, track your credits, and download certificates through our member portal.

  • 24/7 access to materials
  • Progress tracking
  • Downloadable certificates
