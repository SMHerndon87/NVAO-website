About NVAO

Learn about our organization, leadership, and commitment to excellence in ophthalmology

Our History

The Northern Virginia Academy of Ophthalmology was founded in 1985 by a group of dedicated ophthalmologists who recognized the need for a professional organization that would serve the unique needs of eye care practitioners in the Northern Virginia region.

Over the past 35+ years, NVAO has grown from a small group of founding members to a thriving organization of over 120 ophthalmologists, optometrists, and eye care professionals. We have consistently provided high-quality continuing medical education, fostered professional networking, and advocated for excellence in patient care.

Our organization has been instrumental in advancing ophthalmologic practice standards in the region and continues to be a leader in professional development and medical education.

Mission & Values

Mission

To advance the practice of ophthalmology through education, advocacy, and professional collaboration while promoting the highest standards of patient care in Northern Virginia.

Education

Providing comprehensive continuing medical education opportunities and staying current with the latest advances in ophthalmologic care and technology.

Collaboration

Fostering professional relationships and encouraging collaboration among eye care professionals to improve patient outcomes and advance the field.

Patient Care

Maintaining unwavering commitment to excellence in patient care and advocating for the vision health needs of our community.
