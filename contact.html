Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of NVAO?

Membership is open to ophthalmologists, optometrists, and eye care professionals practicing in Northern Virginia. Contact us using the form above or email membership@nvao.org for application details and requirements.

How do I register for events?

Event registration can be completed by contacting us directly or through our member portal. We recommend registering early as space is often limited for our educational programs.

Are CME credits provided for events?

Yes, all our educational events provide AMA Category 1 CME credits. Certificates are issued upon completion of the educational activity and evaluation forms.

What are the membership benefits?

Members enjoy access to high-quality CME programs, professional networking opportunities, educational resources, member directory (pending approval), and exclusive member-only content.

How often does NVAO meet?

We hold monthly clinical meetings, an annual spring symposium, seasonal workshops, and special events throughout the year. Check our events page for the complete calendar.

Can I access educational materials online?

Members have access to our online portal with recorded presentations, educational materials, and resource downloads. Contact us to set up your portal access.
